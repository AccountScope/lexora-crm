KEY RISKS & MITIGATION
================================================================================

RISK 1: "We need SOC 2 to sell"
Reality: Only true for 100+ person firms.
For 5-15 person firms, "SOC 2 in progress" is fine.
First 10 customers won't require it.
By month 6, you'll have it.

RISK 2: "We need 200 integrations"
Reality: First 10 customers need 5 integrations.
Xero, DocuSign, Google Drive, Outlook, Slack = covers 90% of use cases.
Sell the roadmap: "30+ integrations coming in 2026."

RISK 3: "Can't close 10 customers in 6 months"
Reality: 200 outreach emails → 10 customers is 5% conversion.
Industry average for B2B SaaS is 2-3%.
If you're founder-led, personalized, and solve real pain, you'll hit 5%.
Worst case: Takes 9 months instead of 6.

RISK 4: "£30K isn't enough"
Reality: Agreed - this is TIGHT.
Contingency: If you run out, pause and do consulting to fund.
Or: Pre-sell 5 customers before building (£10K upfront each = £50K).

RISK 5: "I don't have time (other projects)"
Reality: This needs 20-30 hours/week for 6 months.
If AccountScope is 30%, VerdictOS 40%, this needs to be 30%.
Either: Pause one project, or hire someone to execute this plan.

RISK 6: "What if customers churn?"
Reality: Law firms switch software slowly.
If you onboard well, support quickly, and show ROI, 95%+ retention is realistic.
Key: Don't oversell. Deliver on promises.
